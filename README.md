## 🚀 Features & Highlights

- **Dynamic Hero Section:** Auto-typing bio showcasing dynamic roles and passions.
- **Honor Roll & STEM Badges:** Displays achievements including **NCC National Level**, **IOQM (3x)**, **NSEC (2x)**, and **ZIO**.
- **Project Showcase:** Highlights major projects like:
  - 🚁 **Quadcopter CC3D Drone** (Hardware & Flight Controls)
  - 🧪 **AASAI** (Olympiad Research)
  - 📚 **WAL** (Book Web Platform)
  - ⚽ **Sorare** (Fantasy Championship Strategy Engine)
  - ♾️ **Life and Conundrums** (Math Paradox Blog)
- **Interactive Vibe Corner:**
  - ⚽ **Pitch & Goal Counter:** An interactive mini-game widget.
  - ☕ **Patrick Jane Corner:** Dynamic quote generator from *The Mentalist*.
- **Glassmorphism Social Widgets:** Quick access to GitHub, Instagram, Discord, Pinterest, and my math blog.
